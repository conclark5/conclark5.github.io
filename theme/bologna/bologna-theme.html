Bologna was very cool and budding with a large young population. It had a very active night life and tons of college aged kids mulling around and drinking. The center of Bologna holds two towers, it is somewhat famous and what the city is known for. Where we stayed looked right out on these towers and was right in the center of the city. It had a very cool morning cafe right next to us, a lot of trendy restaurants for lunch and dinner, and good shopping. This is where we went after Venice, it took about two and a half hours to get there by train. One of the great things about this trip was being able to train from city to city. It makes travel fairly easy and lets you enjoy the scenery as you travel at high speeds.
