One of the best places we went to, was the island of Capri, which is located inside the Gulf of Naples in Southern Italy. We took a boat from a city called Sorrento, that took us around the entirety of the island, which was absolutely spectacular. The rock of the island was crazy, forming endless stalacites, and numerous grottos and caves. The island sits in the Tyrrhenian Sea, the water was extremely blue and clear around the island, and was the naturally warmest body of water I have ever swam in. The water felt like a pool, with the exception of the salt. Around the island there was an absoultely astonishing amount of mansions, what a dream that would be.

This part of the trip was truly one of the best, it was an absoulte perfect paradise here, if you get rid of all the tourists that is.
