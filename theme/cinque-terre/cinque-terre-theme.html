Cinque Terre was absolutely mind-blowing. These five cities are settled along the coast, connected by train tracks, and that's the only way to enter or exit except by boat. It is a UNESCO World Heritage site and has banned all cars, you can walk between each city if the weather permits, and the trails are clear. Unfortunatley, as of recent the trails have been ruined from weather and it is very difficult to walk between. Each city was unique and each was breathtaking, they all had absolutely spectacular views and are an absolute must-see. We went to four of five cities as we were too tired, and it was too dark to go to the last one.
